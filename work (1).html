IdeaOne of my favorite schematics that I wrote was a chemical power plant that uses gravity. I call it the "Grav Gen". Hydrogen in the bottom container is mixed with a special zirconium catalyst. This causes a massive explosion which pushes a gravitational block up on a vertical axis. The kinetic energy of this block propells the air in the top container at very high speeds which then pops open multiple valves due to the ridiculous pressure. It then proceeds into a large wind turbine room. This idea isnt patented and I am not sure how it will do or how much power it will actually create. Its just an interesting concept idea I came up with.

Donotworry! It is not done yet.
